Chicken Katsu 🍗


Description

Japanese-style fried chicken cutlet coated with panko bread crumbs! Serve with your favorite side dishes or plain white rice.

Recipe from here.


Ingredients

  • Chicken breast pounded to 1/2 inch thickness
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 cup oil

Instructions

  1. Season chicken breasts on both sides with salt and pepper. Place flour, beaten egg, and panko crumbs into separate shallow dishes.
  2. Coat chicken breasts in flour, shaking off any excess; dip into egg, and then press into panko crumbs until well coated on both sides.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place chicken in the hot oil, and fry until golden brown, 3 or 4 minutes per side. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain.
